How it works

Three steps. Then Vela works in the background, forever.

1

Record the voice

Patient records 30–60 seconds of natural speech. Or upload old WhatsApp messages, voicemails, family videos. Vela's AI extracts and clones the voice.

2

Vela learns the personality

Region, dialect, slang, humour, energy level. Every message gets rewritten to sound like the patient — not a generic AI voice with their accent on top.

3

Patient communicates, clinicians watch

Patient uses voice, eyes, touch — whatever works. Every interaction generates clinical data. Caregivers stay informed. Doctors get reports without lifting a finger.
